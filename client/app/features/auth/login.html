The Problem

Based on the Terms of Service from Facebook Messenger and Google Hangouts, users agree to allow these companies to read their messages.

The Solution

Unlike other end-to-end encryption chat services, Locket allows users to chat with existing friends on other messaging platforms while protecting user privacy.

Revoke Messages After They're Sent

By clicking on one of the messages you've sent, the message will be destroyed forever. We never save your messages on our server.
